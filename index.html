Dental Innovation Hub

Building the future of dentistry

A student-led initiative sharing innovation in dentistry.

Dental Innovation Hub is a student-led initiative created to share innovation in dentistry with students. We explore existing technology not always taught at dental school, alongside cutting-edge clinical dentistry, and follow us to keep up with what is shaping the future of the profession.

Our mission

We exist to bring students closer to innovation in dentistry by sharing ideas, tools, and experiences that go beyond the standard curriculum.

What we do

  • • Share inspiring stories and emerging ideas.
  • • Highlight technology not always taught at dental school.
  • • Support student curiosity around modern clinical dentistry.

Team

A growing network of dental professionals, designers, and tech enthusiasts working together to make innovation more accessible.

Events

We host evening talks, hands-on lunch and learns, and other events designed to bring innovation in dentistry to students in an engaging way.
